Airport Program

Incheon International Airport is the largest airport in South Korea,
the primary airport serving the Seoul Capital Area, and one of the largest
and busiest airports in the world. Since 2005, it has been rated the best airport
worldwide by Airports Council International every year.

It is also rated as the world's cleanest airport and the world's
best international transit airport by Skytrax.


Airport Facilities for your enjoyable experience


The airport has a golf course, spa, private sleeping rooms, an ice skating rink,
a casino, indoor gardens, video game center and a Museum of Korean Culture.
